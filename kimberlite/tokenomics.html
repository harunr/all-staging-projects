TOKENOMICS

Our Tokenomics are designed with the aim of attracting investors and establishing robust liquidity within the ecosystem.

The value of KimberLite

Price growth is a key objective of our tokenomics. We aim to achieve this by closely controlling the amount of $KIMBER in circulation. By avoiding market over-saturation, we ensure that the value of $KIMBER remains stable and attractive to investors.

Liquidity Protection

190,000,000 tokens (95% of the $KIMBER supply) have been locked for the first 5 years. Only 2% of the supply shall be released each month, therefore protecting the liquidity and stabilising the token price.

Guaranteed Stability

$KIMBER’s value is unrelated to other crypto assets and will be determined by the worldwide transaction volume of diamonds, ensuring long-term stability and growth.

About Us

Team Tokens

Team Tokens

The KimberLite team is adamant about the ecosystem's long-term success. To reflect this dedication, the team’s allocated tokens are locked up for ten years with a further 12 months vesting.

Burn Strategy

Burn Strategy

Having an established corporation as the foundation upon which the ecosystem is built provides the opportunity to re-invest existing profits into the token. One fifth of all operating profits will be used to ‘buy-back’ $KIMBER, until 50% of the supply has been burned.

Lifecycle

Token Lifecycle

$KIMBER has been designed to permanently accrue value by being the utility token of a growing ecosystem. The token will be used for commodity payments, in-game purchases and TNFT sales, among other use cases.
